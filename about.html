Our Heritage

Since 1897, Maison Lumière has crafted exquisite fragrances that capture the essence of luxury and artistry.
Our journey is one of passion, innovation, and unwavering commitment to perfection.

A Legacy of Excellence

For over a century, our master perfumers have been creating olfactory masterpieces that transcend time.
Each fragrance tells a story, each bottle holds a memory, and each scent is a work of art.

1902

Perfume is Born

Founded in Grasse, France, Perfume began with a passion for crafting scents that stir the soul.

1935

Signature Scent

The debut of our classic “Lueur” fragrance captured hearts and became a symbol of timeless sophistication.

1968

Worldwide Presence

Perfume opened its doors in London, Dubai, and Tokyo — becoming a global ambassador of French elegance.

2001

Modern Craftsmanship

Blending tradition with innovation, we introduced sustainable sourcing without compromising on luxury.

Today

Perfume Reimagined

Redefining luxury with bold new blends, Perfume continues to inspire through innovation, elegance, and mindful creation.
